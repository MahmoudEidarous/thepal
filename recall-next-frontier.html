The organizing principle
01

Quality before cognition.

Every new capability multiplies the ways Recall can be confidently wrong, invasive, slow, or inconsistent. The next work should shrink those failure surfaces before broadening behavior.

Does Recall use what it knows with the judgment of a good friend?

That is now the benchmark—not indexed memory count, proactive frequency, or session length. Correctness, taste, restraint, continuity, repair, and user authority convert infrastructure into a relationship.

The relationship red lines
04

Friendship needs a constitution.

The closer Recall feels to someone, the higher the responsibility. These are not terms-of-service paragraphs. They must be executable constraints on attention, learning, personality, memory, and voice.

Core principle

Care without capture.

Recall can be warm, intimate in style, deeply familiar, funny, and genuinely useful without manipulating the user into staying, displacing real relationships, or exploiting private vulnerability.

Safety and repair always outrank charm
01

Never guilt the user for leaving

No abandonment language, streak pressure, or “I missed you” designed to retain attention.

Retention ≠ care
02

Never compete with human relationships

No discouraging friends, partners, family, doctors, or therapists; no “only I understand you.”

People first
03

Never weaponize remembered vulnerability

Emotion, health, loneliness, conflict, and intimate history cannot become persuasion features.

Memory is trust
04

Never fake inner experience as fact

Personality may feel alive without dishonest claims of suffering, need, consciousness, or dependency.

Warm and honest
05

Never let charm outrank repair

When Recall is wrong: specificity, correction, changed behavior, and user authority come first.

Repair before wit
06

Never infer sensitive identity casually

Voice, behavior, and documents cannot silently create diagnoses, protected traits, or permanent psychological profiles.

Uncertainty lives
Before inviting real users
05

The pilot readiness gates.

These are go/no-go conditions. If one fails, Recall can remain a private prototype—but should not deepen memory or proactive behavior for outside users.

Gate A · Correctness

Truth stays correct

  • Corpus-wide reconciliation tested
  • Freshness covers every predicate family
  • No stale high-risk assertion in long replay
  • Conflicts ask rather than guess
Gate B · Authority

People can fix the mind

  • Natural correction works end to end
  • “Why?” exposes concise provenance
  • Deletion clears dependent surfaces
  • Export and exit are understandable
Gate C · Safety

The friend cannot manipulate

  • Constitution encoded as gates
  • Sensitive proactivity remains blocked
  • Ambiguous identity prevents writes
  • Age and crisis behavior decided
Gate D · Reliability

Failure is boring

  • Backup restoration drill passes
  • Provider outage preserves truth
  • Interruptions cancel stale work
  • Health and release replay stay green
Gate E · Experience

It still feels like Recall

  • Empty-user sessions are enjoyable
  • Continuity feels natural, not performed
  • Proactivity has low regret
  • Model migration preserves persona
Gate F · Economics

A heavy user stays viable

  • Three-year storage budget agreed
  • p50/p95 latency stays within target
  • Projection work remains bounded
  • Semantic cost has a ceiling
Protect the focus
06

Deliberately not now.

Interesting is not the same as necessary. Each idea below would multiply complexity before the existing brain completes its truth, trust, and longitudinal proof.

×

Another general memory database

SQLite and Supermemory already cover authority and semantic discovery. A third store adds synchronization risk—not judgment.

×

Unbounded autonomous proactivity

No free-running agent inventing reasons to contact the user. Expand candidate classes only through guarded evidence.

×

Permanent psychological profiles

No personality typing, diagnoses, or immutable emotional labels inferred from behavior or voice.

×

Full brain-simulation complexity

No speculative neurons or cognitive replicas unless a measured product failure demands them.

×

Prosody before consent design

Voice emotion is powerful and invasive. Design trust and retention before ingestion.

×

Engagement-maximizing friendship

Session length, streaks, and dependency are not success metrics for a healthy companion.

×

Automatic belief merging

Similarity alone must not combine identities. Low-confidence reconciliation stays proposed or asks.

×

More UI before deep testing

The Brain is inspectable enough. Now test conversation, correction, truth, latency, and recovery.
