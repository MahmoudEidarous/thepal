The final split
01

Delight and reliability must rise together.

A feature is only worth adding if it reuses the brain already built and makes Recall feel more like a friend. An engineering upgrade is only worth prioritizing if it closes a real path to wrongness, creepiness, latency, or long-term drift.

Hemisphere A · Product magic

Moments the user would actually love.

Co-presence, thoughtful returns, shared rituals, evolving humor, natural correction, and companionship that grows from real history—not novelty demos.

Filter: Does it deepen shared life without becoming performative?
Hemisphere B · Engineering rigor

Work that makes the magic safe.

Truth reconciliation, freshness, thread quality, session handoffs, outcome closure, scale, identity, recovery, and longitudinal evaluation.

Filter: Does it measurably reduce a known failure mode?

The sequence I would actually follow
05

Four waves. No random feature pile.

The goal is to unlock visible magic as soon as its supporting correctness is credible. Each wave deliberately pairs product experience with the engineering that protects it.

Wave A · Foundation precision

Make truth boringly right

Close the gaps that contaminate everything downstream.

  • Write-time slot reconciliation
  • Predicate-specific freshness
  • Thread identity and merge quality
  • Three-year adversarial replay
  • Natural correction experience
Exit · no stale high-risk truth in replay
Wave B · Earned continuity

Make history feel alive

Turn existing continuity surfaces into natural friendship moments.

  • The friend who follows through
  • Weekly and monthly rituals
  • Permissioned initiation contract
  • Session handoff coverage
  • Attention outcome closure
Exit · low proactive regret in real sessions
Wave C · Shared life

Deepen the relationship

Add delight only after timing and repair are trustworthy.

  • Ambient co-presence
  • People-care moments
  • Memory postcards
  • Inside jokes that evolve
  • Relationship evidence wiring
Exit · callbacks and check-ins feel earned
Wave D · New senses

Expand carefully

Cross-device and multimodal features widen both utility and risk.

  • Same friend across channels
  • Photos and voice-note moments
  • Voice prosody
  • Identity and encryption hardening
  • Persona migration evaluation
Exit · consent, deletion, and isolation proven

The brain exists.
Now earn the life around it.

Recall does not need another giant subsystem. It needs a small number of unforgettable experiences built on a smaller number of uncompromising engineering upgrades. Magic without rigor becomes creepy. Rigor without magic becomes infrastructure. The product is the union.
